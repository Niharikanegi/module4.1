Browse bustling Auckland markets

Country Market

Head to the Matakana Village Farmers Market on Saturdays from 8am to 1pm to pick up artisan cheeses, Italian meats, organic chocolate, homemade chutneys, craft beer and other gourmet goodies. On Sundays from 8am to 1pm, The Clevedon Village Farmers Market serves up fresh produce, pastries and baking, and the popular Coatesville Market open Sundays from 10am to 2pm, has around 100 stalls selling food, art, jewellery, homewares and clothing.

A cultrual experience

Join the locals at the buzzing Auckland Night Markets, held at eight different locations across the region on different nights, for traditional street snacks and delicacies from around the world.
